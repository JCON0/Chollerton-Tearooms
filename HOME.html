HERITAGE

Hidden away within the North Tyne Valley, Chollerton tea rooms resides within the quaint village of Chollerton. Established in 1937, the tearooms remains almost untouched by modern architecture, allowing a truly British tea experience. Surrounded by beautiful scenery, Chollerton tearooms suggests a great variety of trips through the North Tyne Valley in which truly remarkable landscapes can be seen.
